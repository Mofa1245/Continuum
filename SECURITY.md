# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

**Do not open public issues for security bugs.** Public disclosure before a fix can increase risk.

Report vulnerabilities privately:

- **Contact:** <PUT TEMP EMAIL OR PLACEHOLDER>
- **Response target:** We aim to acknowledge within 72 hours.

## Scope

We take seriously:

- **Replay integrity** — Tampering or bypass of replay correctness
- **Storage corruption** — Persistence layer or checkpoint integrity
- **Determinism bypass** — Circumvention of deterministic guarantees

If you believe you have found a vulnerability in these areas (or in dependency supply chain affecting them), please report it privately.

## What to Expect

After you report, we will confirm receipt and work with you to understand and address the issue. We will not take legal action against or ask you to waive rights for good-faith security research conducted in line with this policy.
